🔄 **Easy Mode Switching:** Toggle between Default, Bypass (auto-approve), and Sandbox modes with a single click.
🛡️ **Graceful Recovery:** If you accidentally kill the terminal, the extension auto-restarts your session the moment you send your next message.

I kept the UI boxy, clean, and minimal to ensure zero latency and the best UX possible. It passes input directly to the CLI and doesn't capture data, keeping your workflow completely safe.
